Biography

Mahmoud Nazzal is a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Engineering at the New Jersey Institute of Technology, specializing in AI Security and Applications. His research focuses on the security, robustness, and applicability of Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) and Large Language Models (LLMs).

His goal is to develop resilient defenses for AI systems, ensuring their security in real-world applications across different domains like autonomous systems, medical imaging, and generative AI (GenAI) applications.
