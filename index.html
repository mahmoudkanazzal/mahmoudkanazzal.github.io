About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Engineering at the New Jersey Institute of Technology, specializing in AI Security and Applications. My research focuses on the security, robustness, and applicability of Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) and Large Language Models (LLMs).

With several years of experience in academia and industry, I have worked on diverse projects involving adversarial robustness, machine learning for communications, and secure source code generation using advanced AI models. I am passionate about developing innovative solutions that bridge the gap between theory and real-world applications.

Outside of my academic pursuits, I am actively involved in mentoring students, organizing research symposiums, and contributing to the academic community through publications and conferences.

Research Interests

  • Prompt optimization and engineering
  • Secure and functional source code generation
