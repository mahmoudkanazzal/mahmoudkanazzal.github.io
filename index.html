About Me

As a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Engineering at NJIT, I specialize in AI Security and Applications. My research focuses on enhancing the security, robustness, and applicability of Graph Neural Networks (GNNs) and Large Language Models (LLMs).

With a background spanning academia and industry, I've worked on cutting-edge projects in adversarial robustness, secure source code generation, and machine learning for communications. My passion lies in developing innovative solutions that bridge the gap between theoretical advancements and real-world applications.

Beyond research, I actively engage in mentoring students, organizing research symposiums, and contributing to the academic community through publications and conference presentations.
